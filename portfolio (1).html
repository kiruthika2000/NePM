How I Work

Research is nothing without
a creative who can use it.

My approach centers on one belief: the best AI product managers don't just ship models — they ship creative confidence.

01

Deep Discovery with Creatives

I shadow directors, editors, VFX artists, and colorists in their actual production environments. I don't build from assumptions — I build from watching an artist struggle with a 4-hour render that AI could reduce to 4 minutes.

02

Research → Production Bridge

I define evaluation criteria for model quality, adherence, and controllability — then partner with R&D to turn promising prototypes into tools that survive the chaos of real production timelines. 14-week cycles compressed to 6.

03

Right Altitude Communication

Monday: reviewing diffusion model architecture with ML engineers. Wednesday: demoing creative workflows to executive leadership. Friday: aligning compute budgets with infrastructure teams. Same story, different altitude.
